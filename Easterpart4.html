Palm Sunday

Palm Sunday is celebrated when Jesus arrives in Jerusalem and the people put palm leaves to welcome him. This is the start of Lent.


Holy Monday & Tuesday

On Holy Monday and Tuesday, Jesus cursed the fig tree, cleansed the temple, and responded to peoplke questioning his authority. Some observe the anointing of Jesus.


Holy Wednesday

This day was when Judas (One of Jesus's friends) was in a bargain to kill Jesus.


Maundy Thursday

This is when "the last supper" happened the day of Judas's Betrayal too. This was when Jesus had supper with his friends and followers.


Good Friday

This is when Jesus made his sacrifice (crucifixion).


Holy Saturday

This is where the people have mourn about Jesus's death


Easter Sunday

More commonly known as just "Easter"
